Choclate Cake Recipe

Welcome to my recipe page. Here you will find a list of ingredients and instructions to make a delicious chocolate cake.
Follow this simple instructions below to make a delicious chocolate cake.So let's get started.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 1 cup of eggs
  • 1 cup of baking powder
  • 1 cup of baking soda
  • 1 cup of salt
  • 1 cup of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean.
  7. Allow to cool for 10 minutes before removing from pans to cool completely.
